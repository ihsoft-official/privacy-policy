Privacy Policy — Daily Dhikr

Effective date: June 21, 2026

Daily Dhikr ("we", "our", or "us") is a simple spiritual reminder application designed to help users maintain a consistent habit of remembering Allah through daily dhikr, notifications, and personal tracking.

This Privacy Policy explains that your data stays completely on your device and how the app works in a fully offline and local manner.

1. Information we collect

Daily Dhikr does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information remains fully on your device.

  • Dhikr entries — user-added or default dhikr list stored locally
  • History data — your local usage history (if enabled)
  • App settings — preferences such as reminders and notification settings
All data is stored locally using SharedPreferences (or equivalent local storage). Nothing is uploaded to any server.

2. How we use your data

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is used only for:

  • Displaying your dhikr list
  • Managing your custom added or removed dhikr
  • Showing local dhikr history
  • Scheduling local notifications and reminders

3. Data storage

All data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage (SharedPreferences or similar).

We do not use cloud storage, servers, Firebase, or any external database.

4. Third-party services

Daily Dhikr does not use any third-party services such as analytics, authentication, or crash reporting.

  • No Firebase
  • No Google Analytics
  • No external APIs

5. Notifications

The app uses local notifications only to remind users to perform dhikr. These notifications are scheduled and managed entirely on your device.

6. App permissions

Daily Dhikr requests only minimal permissions required for core functionality, such as:

  • Local notifications permission (for reminders)

No storage, contacts, location, or sensitive permissions are used.

7. Data sharing

We do not collect, share, sell, or transmit any user data to any third party. Everything remains completely private and stored only on your device.

8. Children’s privacy

Daily Dhikr is a general spiritual reminder app and does not knowingly collect any information from children or any user.

9. Data control

You have full control over your data at all times. You can:

  • Add or remove dhikr entries
  • Clear your history
  • Reset app data from device settings

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Any changes will be reflected in this document with an updated effective date.
