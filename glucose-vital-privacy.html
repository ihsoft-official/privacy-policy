Privacy Policy — Gluco Vital

Effective date: May 18, 2026

Gluco Vital ("we", "our", or "us") is a health tracking application that allows users to log blood sugar levels, blood pressure (BP), and related health entries. The app also provides charts, insights, and downloadable reports that can be shared with healthcare professionals.

This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled, stored, and protected when you use Gluco Vital.

1. Information we collect

Gluco Vital collects and stores the following types of data to provide its core functionality:

  • Health data (user-generated) — blood sugar readings, blood pressure readings, timestamps, notes, and related entries you manually add.
  • Account information — if sign-in is enabled, basic authentication data (such as email or user ID via Firebase Authentication).
  • App usage data — basic analytics such as feature usage, crash reports, and performance metrics.

We do not sell your personal data. Your health data is stored securely and is only accessible to you.

2. How we use your data

We use collected information to:

  • Store and sync your health records across devices
  • Generate charts and insights based on your entries
  • Create downloadable reports for doctors or personal records
  • Improve app performance and reliability

3. Data storage and security

All user data is securely stored using Firebase (Google Cloud) services. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or loss.

Your health data is encrypted in transit and stored in secure cloud databases. Access is restricted to your authenticated account only.

4. Third-party services

We may use third-party services for authentication, analytics, and crash reporting. These include:

  • Firebase Authentication — https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Firebase Analytics — https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Crashlytics — https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

These providers may collect limited technical data according to their own privacy policies.

5. App permissions

Gluco Vital may request only essential permissions such as internet access for syncing data and optional storage access for exporting reports. No unnecessary permissions are used.

6. Reports and data export

The app allows you to generate health reports (PDF or similar formats) that summarize your logs for sharing with doctors. These reports are generated locally or securely within the app and only shared when you choose to export them.

7. Children’s privacy

Gluco Vital is intended for general health tracking and is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If such data is discovered, it will be deleted immediately.

8. Your data rights

You have full control over your data. You may:

  • View your stored health records at any time
  • Edit or delete entries
  • Delete your account and all associated data

9. Data retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated health records are permanently removed from our systems within a reasonable time frame.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document with a new effective date.
