About Qalb e Qurani

Qalb e Qurani (قلبِ قرآنی) is a comprehensive Islamic companion app designed to help Muslims stay connected to the Quran, track prayers, and find spiritual guidance in everyday moments. Available in English, Urdu, Hindi, and Bengali, it is built for Muslims around the world.
