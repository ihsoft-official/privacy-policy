Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Please read how we handle your data.

🗓 Last updated: June 26, 2026

📌 Overview

Qalb e Qurani does not collect, sell, or share your personal data with third parties. The app is designed to work primarily offline, and any data you generate stays on your device.
Who we are

Qalb e Qurani is developed and maintained by IH Soft. For any privacy concerns, contact us at ih7063768@gmail.com.

Scope

This policy applies to the Qalb e Qurani mobile application available on iOS and Android.

📂 Data We Collect

Reading Progress & Bookmarks

Your Quran reading progress, last read position, and bookmarked ayahs are stored locally on your device only.

Location Data

Your device location is used solely to calculate accurate prayer times. We do not store or transmit your location to any server.

App Settings & Preferences

Your selected language, theme, text size, and notification preferences are saved locally on your device.

Notification Schedule

Reminder times you set (e.g. Daily Quran Reading, Surah Al-Kahf) are stored locally and processed on-device.

🚫 Data We Do NOT Collect

• No account registration or login required

• No personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)

• No usage analytics or behavioral tracking

• No advertising identifiers or third-party ad SDKs

• No data transmitted to external servers by the app itself

🔔 Permissions Used

Location (Approximate)

Used only to calculate prayer times based on your city. Not stored or shared.

Notifications

Used to send you Quran reading reminders and Friday Surah Al-Kahf alerts at your chosen times.

👶 Children's Privacy

Qalb e Qurani is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children or adults. No personal data is collected by the app at any point.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
