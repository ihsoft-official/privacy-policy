Privacy Policy — Gluco Vital

Effective date: May 18, 2026

Gluco Vital ("we", "our", or "us") is a lifestyle health tracking application that allows users to log personal wellness data such as blood sugar readings, blood pressure readings, and related health entries. The app also provides charts, insights, and downloadable reports for personal record keeping and sharing with healthcare professionals.

This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use Gluco Vital.

1. Information we collect

Gluco Vital collects and stores the following types of data to provide core app functionality:

  • User-entered health data — blood sugar readings, blood pressure readings, timestamps, and notes you manually add.
  • Account information — if sign-in is enabled, basic authentication data such as email or user ID via Firebase Authentication.
  • App usage data — basic analytics such as feature usage, crash reports, and performance data.

We do not sell your personal data. Your information is used only to provide app functionality and improve user experience.

2. How we use your data

We use collected information to:

  • Store and sync your records across devices
  • Generate charts and personal insights from your entries
  • Create downloadable reports for personal records or sharing
  • Improve app performance, stability, and user experience

3. Data storage and security

All user data is securely stored using Firebase (Google Cloud). We apply industry-standard security practices to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Your data is encrypted in transit and stored securely in cloud databases. Access is restricted to your authenticated account only.

4. Third-party services

We may use third-party services for authentication, analytics, and crash reporting, including:

  • Firebase Authentication — https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Firebase Analytics — https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Crashlytics — https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

These services may collect limited technical data according to their own privacy policies.

5. App permissions

Gluco Vital may request only necessary permissions such as internet access for syncing data and optional storage access for exporting reports. No unnecessary permissions are used.

6. Reports and data export

The app allows users to generate personal reports (PDF or similar formats) summarizing logged data. These reports are created locally or securely within the app and are only shared when the user chooses to export them.

7. Children’s privacy

Gluco Vital is intended for general wellness tracking and is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If such data is detected, it will be removed promptly.

8. Your data rights

You have full control over your data. You may:

  • View your stored records at any time
  • Edit or delete entries
  • Delete your account and all associated data

9. Data retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems within a reasonable timeframe.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document with an updated effective date.
