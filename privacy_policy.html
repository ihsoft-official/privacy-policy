Privacy Policy — Qalb-e-Qurani

Effective date: October 21, 2025

Qalb-e-Qurani ("we", "our", or "us") provides an app containing a collection of Qur’anic Ayahs (verses) and Duas (supplications) for personal reading and reflection. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

1. Information we collect

Qalb-e-Qurani does not collect any personal information directly from users. However, certain third-party services integrated into the app may collect limited data automatically to provide their functionality:

  • Firebase Analytics — used to understand app usage and performance (e.g., how often a feature is used, device type, crash reports). This helps us improve the app experience. Data collected by Firebase is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Advertising SDKs (e.g., Google AdMob or AppLovin) — used to display in-app advertisements. These services may collect anonymous identifiers (such as Advertising ID), approximate location (if enabled by the device), and information about ad interactions to deliver relevant ads and measure performance.

We do not have access to any personal data collected by these services. All such data is processed according to their respective privacy policies.

2. App permissions

The app requests only permissions necessary for its functionality (for example, local storage access to save bookmarks or preferences). No permission is used to collect or transmit personal data without user consent.

3. Ads and analytics providers

The third-party services we use include (but are not limited to):

  • Google AdMob — https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • AppLovin (if used) — https://www.applovin.com/privacy/
  • Google Firebase — https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

These third-party services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies to show personalized or non-personalized ads, detect fraud, and improve analytics accuracy.

4. Local data & storage

The app may store non-personal data locally (e.g., bookmarks, last read Ayah, theme preferences). This data remains only on your device and is not uploaded or shared.

5. Accuracy and content disclaimer

We strive to provide accurate Arabic text and translations of Qur’anic verses and Duas. However, as the content is manually curated and translated by humans, unintentional errors or variations may occur.

If you find any error in Arabic text, translation, or meaning, please contact us. We will review and correct it in future updates.

Qalb-e-Qurani is designed for learning, reflection, and spiritual benefit. While every effort is made for accuracy, we cannot guarantee that all text and translations are error-free.

6. Children’s privacy

The app is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your region). If you believe that we have inadvertently received such data, please contact us immediately and we will remove it.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes (e.g., new data collection or additional services) will be updated here with a new effective date.
